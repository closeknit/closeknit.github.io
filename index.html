Everyone should have an opportunity to express themselves. Through creativity, laughter and play. Close-Knit provide inclusive dance and art classes that enhance social interaction, provide mental stimulation and promote general well-being for adults aged 18+.

All classes are inclusive and celebrate what each individual can achieve. Working 1:1 as well as facilitating group work Close-Knit are able to provide a unique experience regardless of age and ability.
